## Evidence-quality rules

- **Strong**: official/government, major research report, reputable consulting survey with methodology, standards body.
- **Medium**: analyst forecasts, paywalled reports with public summaries, industry surveys where methodology is visible.
- **Vendor**: useful for product surface, buyer language, and feature patterns; not enough alone for market-size or ROI claims.
- **Weak**: SEO summaries, unsourced statistics, leaderboard articles without primary benchmark links.
